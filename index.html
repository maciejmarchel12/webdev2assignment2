Mythic Dungeon International

The {{{MDI}}} or MDI for short is a tournament for teams of 5 players competing to get a high key done agaisnt another team in the popular Western MMO {{{game}}} developed and published by Activision-Blizzard you can read more about this after clicking the button below.

More about Mythic Dungeon International

The MDI is another form of esports for the game, in 2021 it got restructered and has 4 cups with the prize pool being $20,000 in total, as a new expansion launched for the game it is season 1 once again the seasonal affix which is part of the whole mythic + endgame content for season 1 is prideful, a enemy spawns in after killing a certain % of enemies and if killed the players get empowered for a minute while this one can be posistive there are 3 others which slow the players down that change each week.

Teams have to reach 180 points and the winning team takes $8,000 with the 2nd team taking $4,500 while reaching 150 points, the 3rd team reaching 120 points gets $3,000, 4th place with 90 points earned gets $1,500, 5th and 6th place reaching 60 points gets $1,000, 7th place reaching 30 points gets $500 along with 8th place

The format is 5v5 two teams race each other in a best of 3 runs, its run and broadcasted on the weekends with the days being Friday and Sunday with the venue being strictly online on the official world of warcraft channel on youtube
